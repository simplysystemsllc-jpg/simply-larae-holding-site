--------------------------------------------------
LEGAL POSITIONING AND BUSINESS GUARDRAILS
--------------------------------------------------

The website and product must be structured so that Simply LaRae is clearly positioned as an independent beauty advisory and consulting platform.

Critical positioning rules:
- Simply LaRae is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any retailer or brand unless explicitly stated
- Simply LaRae recommendations must be based on user inputs, suitability, and beauty logic, not retailer preference
- Simply LaRae does not guarantee pricing, availability, shipping, inventory, or product performance from third-party sellers
- Product links are for customer convenience only
- Purchases made through third-party links are between the customer and the retailer
- Simply LaRae must remain retailer-agnostic and recommendation-first
- Do not imply any partnership with Sephora, Ulta, or any other beauty seller unless explicitly true
- Do not use language suggesting official authorization, representation, endorsement, or certification by any retailer or brand
- If exact legal protections vary by jurisdiction, note that site pages are informational templates and should be reviewed by counsel before launch

--------------------------------------------------
NON-AFFILIATE INDEPENDENCE
--------------------------------------------------

Simply LaRae does NOT operate as an affiliate marketing platform by default.

The platform should be designed so that product links can point directly to trusted retailers or official brand websites without requiring affiliate relationships.

Recommendations must remain independent across the marketplace and based on what best serves the customer, regardless of retailer.

Use the following positioning language throughout the site where appropriate:

"Simply LaRae provides personalized beauty recommendations based on your inputs, preferences, and analysis results. Product links direct you to trusted third-party retailers or brand websites where purchases are completed independently."

"Simply LaRae is an independent beauty advisory platform and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or partnered with any retailer or brand unless explicitly stated."

If affiliate links are ever added later, the architecture must support clear disclosure, but affiliate logic should remain disabled by default.
